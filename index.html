About Me

Hello there! I am a passionate and ambitious Third Year Computer Science Engineering student at Pune University, specializing in Quantum Computing. My journey into the world of coding began during middleschool when I was introduced to C++. It was an exhilarating experience to realize that I could control computers by typing a few lines of special code. This fascination with coding only grew stronger as I progressed.

However, my true "aha" moment came when I started my college journey. It was then that I was first exposed to the captivating world of Neural Networks. Learning about this cutting-edge technology ignited a fire within me, and I delved headfirst into the world of Deep Learning. Since then, I have embarked on numerous projects and completed various certification courses, all dedicated to mastering the intricacies of Neural Networks.

The thrill of continuous learning keeps me motivated. Each day brings new surprises and challenges, and I firmly believe that one should never stop learning. Life has taught me that just when you feel you've reached your limits, there's always something more to explore and achieve.

As I move forward in my academic and professional journey, I eagerly embrace opportunities to contribute to the ever-evolving field of Quantum Computing and AI. With a relentless desire to grow, I am committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible.

Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. If you share the same passion for innovation and technology, I'd love to connect with you and explore how we can create a better future together.
